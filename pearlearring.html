Girl with a Pearl Earring

Artist: Johannes Vermeer

Year of Creation: c. 1665

Girl with a Pearl Earring is an oil painting by Dutch Golden Age painter Johannes Vermeer. The painting is a "tronie," a Dutch word for a painting of an individual that isn't intended to be a portrait. It is renowned for its soft light, vivid contrast, and the enigmatic gaze of the subject.
